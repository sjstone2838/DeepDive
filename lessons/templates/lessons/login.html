Study

DeepDive was founded on the belief that you learn best when you’re constantly problem-solving. No kicking back and simply clicking through videos. At DeepDive, you’ll be answering questions, working through examples, and getting real-time feedback from the start

You can enroll in as many courses as you like, for free. Learn about an existing passion or find a new one. Work on your own schedule and at your own pace. Pay attention to the feedback you get along the way to pass your tests and keep on unlocking new content

Teach

Have a subject you want to teach the world about? Have a group of classmates, employees, or friends you want to teach? Want to verify that students are actually learning your material and gather data on their progress and satisfaction? And want to do all that for free? DeepDive is for you!

Use our creator tools to create courses with multiple modules. Fill your modules with a rich set of content – text, videos, documents, quizzes, etc. End each module with a test to gauge your students’ performance and ensure students unlock new material only once they’ve mastered what comes before it

Analyze

As students enroll in courses you have published, you'll accumulate performance and satisfaction data. DeepDive provides you a number of ways to synthesize this data. Understand how your students are learning and take a data-driven approach to improving your courses
